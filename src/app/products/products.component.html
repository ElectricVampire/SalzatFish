    Native to Central and south America and also called Pacific white shrimp. It is important aquaculture species reared in most parts of the world. Adult live in the ocean at depth up to 72 metres, young ones live in estuaries. Success of Vannamei is due to better growth rate, feed conversion efficiency, adaptation to variation in temperature, salinity and other environmental conditions. Brood stock of Vannamei are usually imported from Hawaii & South America.
