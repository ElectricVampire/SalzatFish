Salzat Fish Limited farms is a INDIA-based company leveraging nextgeneration eco-friendly aquaculture technology. The company is currently considering sites for a hatchery, fish farm facilities, and a state-of-the-art processing plant in coastal INDIA.

Salzat Fish Limited farms is privately funded and has so far not been actively seeking external capital.

The company has however already been contacted by several investors that have voiced an interest in the project, and Salzat Fish Limited farms will together with advisors enter a dialog with potential strategic investors for future capitalization of the company.
