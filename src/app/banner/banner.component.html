IT ALL STARTS WITH ROE

The Roe is the start of the journey. First class genetics is used to achieve a superior product. The roe is fertilized at the Salzat Fish Limited farms hatchery where it is kept in tubs on land in cold fresh water. After 60 days the freshly hatched prawns is now called yolk sac fry, from its stomach sack that it nourished from for four to six weeks. When it begins to absorb feed, it is moved from the hatchery to a larger tub.

THE ROE LATER GROWS INTO A SMOLT PRAWNS

The smolt is grown in a land based grow out facility in a temperature and bio controlled environment that ensures a healthy fish with strong growth rate and an excellent platform before being transported to the ocean.

Get Started

THE SMOLT PRAWNS IS PLACED IN OCEAN BASED CLOSED PENS

When the smolt is ready it is transported from land to the Ocean where it is placed in an eco-friendly Closed Pen. The closed pen creates an optimal environment for the prawns to grow.

  • Eliminates fish escapes and separates the fish completely from predators
  • Reduces the need for medicine/chemicals
  • Eliminates sea lice
  • Controls waste from reaching the ocean

AND THERE IS GROWS INTO A LARGE PRAWNS

When the prawn reaches the desired size, it is collected onto a modern harvesting vessel and it is now ready for a short trip back on land to be processed by our dedicated team.
